Meteor's Facebook Bot

This is an experiment to integrate Facebook Page Bots SDK (announced at Facebook's F8 Developer Conference) into a Meteor app that acts as the server to process the incoming and outcoming messages received by the Bots. You can find the example here, the quick tutorial here and the codebase here.

The app runs on Meteor 1.3.1 and requires two additional packages to be installed; nimble:restivus and http. The app is hosted at Heroku (you can read how to deploy a Meteor app to Heroku here).
